TONKATSU

Originally from Japan, Tonkatsu or Pork Cutlets is one of the most famous dish in the world. It is normally composed of chicken or pork that is then seasoned and coated with flour, eggs, and breadcrumbs. This recipe only takes 30 minutes to complete is one of the best dishes you may have. With its versatility, it can be used to be the base of different dishes such as Curry and Katsudon.

Ingredients needed:

  • Pork/Chicken Cutlet
  • Breadcrumbs
  • flour
  • eggs
  • Salt & Pepper
    •

Step by Step in Making Tonkatsu

  1. Add Seasoning to the meat, flour, and breadcrumbs
  2. Beat the eggs
  3. Dip the meat into the seasoned flour & shake off any excess
  4. Coat the meat in the egg drench & shake off any excess
  5. Put the meat in the breadcrumbs and coat it properly & shake off any excess
  6. Pan Fry/Deep Fry until golden brown
