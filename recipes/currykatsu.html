Katsu Curry

Originally from Japan, Katsu Curry or Pork Cutlets is considered to be the best dish in the world. It is primary composed of different flavours that come from a packet you order from Japan. It is a dish best served with a pan scrambled egg on the side.

Ingredients needed:

  • Tonkatsu
  • Potato
  • White Onion
  • Carrots
  • Golden Curry (Mild)[You can order this from any store i.e. amazon]
    •

Step by Step in Making Katsu Curry

  1. On low heat, caramelize the white onion
  2. Once, caramelized add the potato and carrots & saute until they have color.
  3. Add 750ml water and bring it to a boil until the potatoes & carrots are soft
  4. Once soft, add 4 cubes of the curry powder and stir for 5 minutes
  5. Once any excess powder has been mixed, add rice to a bowl
  6. Add the tonkatsu on top, then add the sauce
