Katsudon

A simple dish from Japan, it is a tonkatsu in a bowl with sweet sauce and scrambled eggs on top. It is one of my most favorite dishes as it easy to make and has restaurant quality.

Ingredients needed:

  • Tonkatsu
  • Kikoman
  • White Onion
  • Dashi
  • Mirin (Rice Wine)
  • Sugar
    •

Step by Step in Making Katsudon

  1. On low heat, caramelize the white onion
  2. Once, caramelized add Kikoman and Mirin (4:1 cup ratio respectively).
  3. Stir for a little bit, and then add a teaspoon of dashi
  4. Let it boil for 5 minutes, then add sugar. The sweetness depends on you!
  5. Let it boil for another 5 minutes; add rice to your bowl already
  6. Add the tonkatsu on top, then add the sauce and add beaten eggs (they will cook with the heat)
